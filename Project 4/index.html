Who are we?

We are a small pop-up crayon cafe business. About once per season we set up a little Cafe in an interesting location for a week. We focus on creating pleasant atmospheres in unique places. Come find us!
Our crayons
To help foster creative conversation, all of our tables are covered in paper and we make custom scented crayons for people to draw with as they talk and drink their coffee.
