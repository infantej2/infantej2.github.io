About Us

We believe people need space to create. But in the attention economy, noise and distractions are eroding that space. Our name originates from the 53 centimeters that connect head, heart, and hand—the personal canvas for creation.

For as long as they’ve been creating, humans have found that tools—of bone, bronze, or silicon —are vital to making ideas tangible. As a company, our goal has been to create digital tools that are designed to encourage human creativity and create the space to make great things.

FiftyThree—the Space to Create.

Our products focus on the essential: creating an environment of focus, one that reduces noise and allows visual thinkers’ ideas to flow.
